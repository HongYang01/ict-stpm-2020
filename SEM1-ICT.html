Central Processing Unit (CPU)

CPU consists of two main components :

Control Unit (CU)
  • is to direct the CPU to process data
Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU)
  • can perform all arithmetic & logical operations

How Data is Processed by CPU

Steps :
  1. Instructions are transferred from the storage device to the memory, when a program is started.
  2. User input data needed by a program.
  3. Control Unit (CU) interprets & executes instruction the memory.
  4. Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU) performs calculations on the data in the memory.
  5. Information is then stored in the memory.

Principle of Animation

What is animation ?

animation makes static presentations come alive.
Principle Explanation
Squash & Stretch illusion of weight & volume to a character as it moves
Anticipation helps to prepare the viewer for what's about to happen. (starting to run)
Staging to show the character's attitude, mood, reaction or idea.
Straight Ahead & Pose to Pose Animation to create fluid & realistic movements
Follow Through & Overlapping Action objects come to a standstill after being in motion, different parts of the object will stop at different rates
Slow-Out & Slow-In start moving slowly, before gaining momentum and speeding up. And vice versa
Arcs For example, when you toss a ball into the air, it follows a natural arc as the effects of the Earth's gravity act upon it.
Secondary Action used to support or emphasize the main action going on within a scene.
Timing More drawings between poses slow & smooth the action. While fewer drawings make the action faster and crisper.
Exaggeration to make the animation more dynamic and less boring
Solid Drawing understanding form and anatomy, weight and volume, and lights and shadows.
Appeal This includes having an easy-to-read design, solid drawing, and a personality.
